Cooking With Mushrooms

Who knew they could be so delicious?!?!

Cooking Mushrooms is super easy and fun to do! Here's a delicious recipe using a Lion's Mane mushroom (pictured above)

  • in a skillet at medium heat add 2 Tablespoons olive oil.
  • -Add one small diced onion and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  • -Tear lions mane into bite size pieces keeping the spines intact. Add a cup of torn lions mane into the skillet. Cook for 10-12 minutes until mushroom starts to brown.
  • Add 1 Tablespoon butter and turn heat onto medium high for 2-5 minutes. Be sure to stir frequently and not burn the mushrooms.
  • Add Kale and a splash of water, cover and cook until kale is wilted.
  • Add one tablespoon of lemon juice and balsamic vinegar.

    • Can be served as a side or placed on top of rice or quinoa as a main course.
